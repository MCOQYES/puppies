Uuree & Marlon

Two worlds. One meeting point.

Intro

Hi Uuree,
I made this small space for us — simple, quiet, and honest.
We come from opposite sides of the world: you from Mongolia, me from Colombia.
And somehow, our paths crossed in Madrid.

What I Feel for You

You feel close, even from far away.
Your presence softens my days.
Your words stay with me longer than you know.

What I Dream With You

I dream of simple things: walking beside you, sharing a meal, hearing your laugh without a screen in between.
I want to learn your world — and show you mine.

Where Our Worlds Meet

Between Mongolia and Colombia, there are thousands of kilometers.
But all those lines on the map bend toward one point: Madrid.

Our Future

I don’t know what the future looks like.
But I know what I want: a future where you’re part of my days, not just my messages.

Midpoint

Between your sky and mine,
there is a line the world cannot see.

It crosses oceans,
mountains,
time zones,
and still finds its way
to a single place.

A midpoint.
A city.
A moment.
You and me.

Two distant worlds,
quietly choosing
the same direction.
